About the Project_

This project documents the development of a physical, chemistry escape room designed specifically for high school, technical, and undergraduate students.

Each puzzle was carefully mapped around real curriculum topics, challenging players to apply their recent classroom knowledge to crack codes and escape. Guided by two faculty advisors, every enigma was prototyped and tested to balance academic rigor with immersive, cooperative gameplay.

Built as an interactive educational tool, this page highlights how active learning and gamification can transform complex science into an unforgettable classroom adventure.

20 Min Game Time
5–8 Players per Session
~2yr Development

The Challenges

Project Timeline

Phase 01 · Completed

Curriculum Mapping

Analyzed recent chemistry topics taught in High School, Technical, and Higher Education levels to design tailored puzzle concepts.

Phase 02 · Completed

Puzzle Prototyping

Designed chemical enigma cards, visual cryptography, and logic flows utilizing specific curriculum content for each student group.

Phase 03 · In Progress

Full Room Construction

Assembling a physical room, locking mechanisms, and color-coded clue files for live deployment.

Phase 04 · Upcoming

Multi-Level Playtesting

Running blind playthroughs with students from different academic levels to calibrate puzzle difficulty and engagement.

Phase 05 · Concept

Multiple Rooms

Establishing the concept of interconnected multiple rooms for advanced learning..
